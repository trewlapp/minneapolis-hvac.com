Our HVAC Services

Furnace Repair & Installation

Expert furnace repair, replacement, and installation services. We service all major brands and models. Whether your furnace stopped working in the middle of a Minnesota winter or you need an energy-efficient upgrade, our certified technicians will restore warmth to your home fast.

AC Repair & Installation

Professional air conditioning repair, installation, and replacement. We diagnose and fix cooling issues quickly to keep your home comfortable during hot Minneapolis summers. Energy-efficient AC installations available with flexible financing options.

HVAC Maintenance

Preventive maintenance plans to keep your heating and cooling systems running at peak efficiency year-round. Regular tune-ups extend equipment life, improve energy efficiency, and prevent costly breakdowns. Schedule your seasonal maintenance today.

Emergency HVAC Service

HVAC emergencies don't wait for business hours. We provide prompt emergency repair services for sudden system failures, no heat situations, and AC breakdowns. Call us anytime — we respond fast to restore your home's comfort.

Heat Pump Services

Professional heat pump installation, repair, and maintenance. Heat pumps provide efficient heating and cooling in one system. Our technicians are trained on the latest heat pump technology and can help you choose the right system for your home.

Thermostat Installation

Smart thermostat and programmable thermostat installation services. Upgrade to a Wi-Fi enabled thermostat for better temperature control, energy savings, and remote access. We install and configure all major brands including Nest, Ecobee, and Honeywell.

Indoor Air Quality

Improve your home's indoor air quality with air purifiers, humidifiers, and ventilation solutions. We help reduce allergens, dust, and pollutants so your family breathes cleaner, healthier air year-round. Custom solutions for any home size.
